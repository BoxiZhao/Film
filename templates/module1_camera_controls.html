Camera Controls

Click (or tap) a control name to learn more:

Film advance lever – moves to the next empty frame.

Shutter speed dial – sets exposure time (e.g. 1/60 s).

Aperture ring – adjusts lens opening to control depth of field.

Film rewind crank – rewinds film back into cartridge.

ISO dial – tells the meter the speed of the loaded film.
