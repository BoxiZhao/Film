Choosing a Camera

Select a camera that matches your goals and budget:

  • Beginner – an all‑mechanical 35 mm SLR (e.g. Nikon FM2, Pentax K1000) is reliable and inexpensive.
  • Point‑and‑shoot – fully automatic compacts are great for travel candids.
  • Experimentation – a medium‑format TLR or rangefinder offers larger negatives and shallow depth‑of‑field.
