Choosing Film

Film choice influences colour palette, grain, and exposure latitude.

  • ISO 100–200 – fine grain, best for bright daylight or tripod work.
  • ISO 400 – versatile all‑round stock for most lighting.
  • ISO 800+ – suitable for low‑light or fast action, with more visible grain.



You can manually enter attributes to match any film.
