Step 2. Development

Pour the diluted developer into the tank
Agitate by gently inverting the tank

Using {{ film_type }} at ISO {{ iso }} with {{ developer }}:

1. Dilute the developer by 1:50.

2. Pour into the tank (maintain 20–24 °C).

3. Develop for 9 minutes, agitating for 10 s every minute.
