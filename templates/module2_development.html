Step 2. Development

Using Ilford HP5 Plus at ISO 400 with Rodinal 1 : 50:

  1. Dilute the developer 1 part Rodinal to 50 parts water.
  2. Pour into the tank (maintain 20–24 °C).
  3. Develop for 9 minutes, agitating for 10 s every minute.
