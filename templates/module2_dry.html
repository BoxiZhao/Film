Step 5: Wash and Dry

 

Wash thoroughly with running water

Hang film to dry in a dust-free environment

 

Wash the negatives in running water for 5 minutes. Add a wetting agent in the final rinse to prevent water spots.

Gently shake off excess water and hang the film strips to dry in a dust-free environment for at least 2 hours.
