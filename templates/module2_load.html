How to use a film camera

Step 1: Load the film

 

In complete darkness or inside a changing bag, load the exposed roll onto a spiral reel and place it inside the developing tank. Ensure the lid locks securely to maintain light-tightness.

Caution

Any stray light before fixing will fog the undeveloped negatives.
